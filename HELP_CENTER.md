AVS(Address Verification Service,地址验证服务)是一种用于验证持卡人账单地址信息的风控机制。

**作用**:
- 提升交易安全性
- 防止欺诈交易
- 保护商家和持卡人利益

**UeeCard支持情况**:
- 支持标准AVS验证
- 自动匹配账单地址
- 提高支付成功率

**使用建议**:
- 填写真实的账单地址
- 确保地址格式正确
- 使用英文填写

**第二步: 核对交易信息**
- ✅ 检查卡号是否正确

- ✅ 检查账单地址

**第三步: 确认商户与消费场景**
- ⚠️ 部分商户可能不支持虚拟卡
- ⚠️ 部分商户对卡BIN有限制
- ⚠️ 部分商户对地区有限制

**第四步: 尝试更换方式**
- 更换卡段
- 更换支付时间
- 更换网络环境
- 重新绑定卡片

**第五步: 联系客服**
如问题持续,请提交工单并提供:
- 商户名称
- 交易时间
- 交易金额

### 4. 不同卡段的汇率是否不一样?

不同卡段**不设独立汇率**,所有交易均以USD结算。

**汇率差异原因**:
- 交易币种不同(非USD交易按实时汇率折算)
- 交易时间不同(市场汇率波动)
- 商户或通道差异(预授权与清算金额不同)

## Payments & Consumption

### 1. How to solve merchant declining payment?

When payment is declined, it's usually not a card issue. Follow these steps:

**Step 1: Check Card Status**
- ✅ Confirm card is not frozen
- ✅ Confirm sufficient balance
- ✅ Confirm not exceeding limit

**Step 2: Verify Transaction Information**
- ✅ Check card number is correct
- ✅ Check expiration date and CVV
- ✅ Check billing address
- ✅ Complete 3DS verification (if applicable)

**Step 3: Confirm Merchant and Scenario**
- ⚠️ Some merchants may not support virtual cards
- ⚠️ Some merchants have card BIN restrictions
- ⚠️ Some merchants have regional restrictions

**Step 4: Try Alternative Methods**
- Change card segment
- Change payment time
- Change network environment
- Rebind card

**Step 5: Contact Support**
If problem persists, submit a ticket with:
- Merchant name
- Transaction time
- Transaction amount
- Decline screenshot
- Card segment information

> 💡 **UeeCard provides 24/7 customer support**, ready to help you anytime!

3. **Support Assistance** - If refund is delayed, submit ticket for investigation

**Refund Time**:
- Generally: 3-7 business days
- Maximum: No more than 30 business days

Different card segments **do not have independent exchange rates**, all transactions are settled in USD.
